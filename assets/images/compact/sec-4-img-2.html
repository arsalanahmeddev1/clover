Frequently Asked Questions

The Compact payment terminal delivers essential payment functions, including surcharging and cash discounts. Compact merchants will also have access to Clover dashboard features, like Rapid Deposit and Clover Capital.

The Compact does not support common POS features like menu management, inventory management, and order management. Merchants seeking full POS features should consider Clover devices such as Station Solo, Station Duo, Mini, and Flex.

Compact can be used across many industries, but is best suited for businesses seeking a cost-effective credit card payment terminal that is streamlined, fast, and user-friendly, including: retail, quick service restaurants, salons, medical providers, professional services, and personal services.

For net-new merchants, they can only use it with the Payments Plan. For existing merchants, they can use their Compact countertop card terminal with any software plans that Clover has.

Compact supports a wide array of payment options to cater to diverse customer preferences, which include:

  • Credit and debit cards: Accept all of the major credit and debit cards, such as Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
  • Mobile payments: Enable popular mobile wallet options, such as Apple Pay® and Google Wallet™ for quick, contactless transactions.
  • EMV chip cards: Help safeguard transactions with chip card technology, reducing the risk of fraud.
  • Contactless payments: Allow customers to pay by simply tapping their cards or waving devices over the contactless payment terminal.
  • Gift cards: Process and redeem gift cards easily, enhancing customer engagement and loyalty.

Compact purchase includes the payment terminal machine, QSG, charging adapter, and pre-Installed SIM. You have the option to add the following accessory:

  • Compact Swivel Stand: Mini swivel stand with a Compact stand adapter for countertop terminals.
